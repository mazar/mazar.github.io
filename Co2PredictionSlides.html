Why Co2 Prediction

Want to predict amount of Co2 emission produced from your road trip? You can use Co2 Predictor! You only need to know fuel type and engine capacity of the vehicle and this app gives you amount of Co2 produced per kilometer traveled.

Supported Fuel Types

Following fuel types are supported:

unique.data.Fuel.Type.
1 Diesel
2 Diesel Electric
3 Electricity / Diesel
4 Electricity / Petrol
5 Petrol
6 Petrol Electric
7 Petrol Hybrid
